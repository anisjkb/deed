Privacy Policy

At Deed, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal information collected through our websites, property showrooms, project visits, and other interactions related to our real-estate developments and services.

  1. Information We Collect
    We collect information you provide when you inquire about a property, book an appointment, register interest, or subscribe to updates. This may include your name, contact details, location preferences, and communication records. We may also gather limited technical data such as browser type and device identifiers to enhance site performance.
  2. Use of Personal Data
    We use your information to respond to inquiries, schedule property viewings, share project details, process reservations, send promotional updates (where permitted), and improve our products and customer experience.
  3. Data Security
    We employ industry-standard safeguards and restricted data access protocols to protect your personal information against unauthorized use, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  4. Cookies and Analytics
    Our website uses cookies and analytics tools to understand visitor behavior, track marketing campaign effectiveness, and improve navigation. You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer not to be tracked.
  5. Sharing of Information
    We do not sell or rent your data. Information may be shared only with trusted partners such as sales associates, digital service providers, or marketing agencies engaged to assist in property promotions, all bound by confidentiality agreements.
  6. Third-Party Websites
    Our platforms may include links to financing institutions, interior designers, or related partners. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.
  7. Your Rights
    You may request access to your personal data, corrections of inaccurate details, or deletion of records that are no longer required for legitimate business purposes, subject to applicable laws.
  8. Data Retention
    We retain customer information only as long as necessary for ongoing communication, legal compliance, or warranty and after-sales obligations.
  9. Privacy for Minors
    Our marketing and property offerings are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.
  10. Policy Updates
    We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in regulation, technology, or company practices. Updated versions will be published on this page with a new effective date.
